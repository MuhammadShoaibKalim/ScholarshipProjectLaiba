Cyber Secuirity

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It is also known as information technology security or electronic information security. The term applies in a variety of contexts, from business to mobile computing, and can be divided into a few common categories.

Learning Outcomes:
  1. Protects system against viruses, worms, spyware and other unwanted programs.
  2. Forecasting (sales, revenue and customer retention)
  3. Pattern detection weather patterns, financial market patterns, etc.
  4. Minimizes computer freezing and crashes.
  5. Gives privacy to users

Why Become a CyberSecurity?

Cybersecurity has the two key logistical advantages for a strong career: Low to no unemployment and solid compensation. Plus, if you choose this path, you will always have room to grow. You will continually be learning new skills and working to understand new technologies.

Digital Marketing

What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing, also called online marketing, is the promotion of brands to connect with potential customers using the internet and other forms of digital communication. This includes not only email, social media, and web-based advertising, but also text and multimedia messages as a marketing channel. Essentially, if a marketing campaign involves digital communication, it's digital marketing

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Marketing Automation
  • Email Marketing
  • Digital Public Relations (PR)
  • Inbound Marketing

To understand what digital marketing is, it's also important to know some common areas of digital marketing:

Digital Marketer Focus

Digital marketers may focus on one area that they specialize in, although they are more likely to hold a variety of skills related to digital marketing. Niche areas in the digital marketing sphere include content marketing focused on search engine optimization (SEO), copywriting, pay-per-click (PPC) advertising including online advertising like Google ads, social media marketing, as well as email marketing. Along with this, you may also specialize in data and analytics.

  • Inbound Marketing
  • Sponsored Content
  • Content Marketing
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Pay Per Click (PPC)/Paid Search Marketing
  • Affiliate Marketing
